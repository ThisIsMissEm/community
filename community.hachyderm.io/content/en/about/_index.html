Safe Space

Hachyderm is built on empathy and respect. We support marginalized communities.

We work hard to support black communities. We understand autism and neurodivergence.

We work hard to support queer, lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and all members of the sexual attraction and identity alphabet.
